About Seekivo

Seekivo is a passion project born out of a simple need for a reliable and easy-to-use age and date calculator. We believe that tools should be straightforward, fast, and, most importantly, respectful of your privacy.

Unlike many online calculators, our service runs entirely in your browser. This means your data never leaves your device and is never stored on our servers. You can use Seekivo with confidence, knowing that your personal information is completely secure.

Our goal is to provide a clean, ad-free experience that helps you get the information you need in seconds. Whether you're planning a project, celebrating a birthday, or just curious about the time between two moments, Seekivo is here to help.

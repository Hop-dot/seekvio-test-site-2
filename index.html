About Seekvio

Seekvio is a passion project born out of a simple need for a reliable and easy-to-use age and date calculator. We believe that tools should be straightforward, fast, and, most importantly, respectful of your privacy.

Unlike many online calculators, our service runs entirely in your browser. This means your data never leaves your device and is never stored on our servers. You can use Seekvio with confidence, knowing that your personal information is completely secure.

Our goal is to provide a clean, ad-free experience that helps you get the information you need in seconds. Whether you're planning a project, celebrating a birthday, or just curious about the time between two moments, Seekvio is here to help.

Privacy Policy

At Seekvio, your privacy is our top priority. Our service is designed with a "privacy-by-design" approach, meaning we do not and cannot collect your personal data.

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any dates or other information you enter into the calculator.
  • Local Processing: All calculations are performed in your web browser. This ensures that the data you input never leaves your device.
  • No Cookies or Tracking: We do not use cookies, trackers, or any other third-party services to monitor your usage of this website.
  • Anonymity: You can use Seekvio completely anonymously, without fear of your data being compromised.

By using Seekvio, you are choosing a tool that respects your privacy. If you have any questions about our policy, please feel free to contact us.
